ABOUT US

IQRA Educational Institute run under IQRA Educational Trust. We starts at the first lockdown that happened in India (1st quarter of 2020).

We provide online classes especially for kids (Age 4-10). Initially we started Abacus training through Google meet classes with Individual care & group classes also.We started with 5 students, now it grows as 70 set of students.

We train the students to participate all India National & state level competition to show their best in every competition. Our students were achieved Champion of champion in National & State level competition and show their best along all over participants.
As of now, we provide a training on below courses.

● Abacus

● Spoken English

● Handwriting

● Tamil

● Drawing

● Montessori Teacher Training

Our motive is our students to be shine & sparkle like a star in Various fields, they have to develop their talents in this challenging world. And to acquire knowledge on various fields of education. Iqra Education gives a facilitating learning, or the acquisition of knowledge, skills, values, morals, beliefs, habits, and personal development.
